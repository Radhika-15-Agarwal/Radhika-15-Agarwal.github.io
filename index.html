My Favorite Recipe: Chocochip Cookies

These soft and chewy chocolate cookies have an intense chocolate flavor and almost brownie-like texture. They're one of my all-time faves!

Description

These chocolate chip cookies are the perfect combination of chewy and crispy, with a rich, buttery flavor and plenty of chocolate chips. They're easy to make and always a hit!

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
  • Yield: 24 cookies

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth and creamy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water, then add to the batter along with the salt.
  5. Gradually stir in the flour, and mix until well combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto an ungreased baking sheet, leaving space between each cookie.
  8. Bake for about 10 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
